Current release version: **0.250.001**

Workspace documents gained card and folder-card views, selectable conversation-linked deletion, generated artifact promotion, group share approvals, branding heroes, stats exports, and retention cleanup.

## User Side

Users can browse workspaces in List, Cards, Folders, and Folders + Cards views, promote generated artifacts, review share approvals, export stats, and choose whether linked workspace documents are deleted with conversations.

## Admin Side

Admins and workspace managers govern public and group workspace approvals, branding, retention, stats windows, document sharing, and generated artifact promotion behavior.

## Why It Matters

Document-heavy workspaces need predictable lifecycle choices from upload to approval, reuse, reporting, and deletion.

## How to Try It

1. Open Personal, Group, or Public Workspace documents.
2. Switch between List, Cards, Folders, and Folders + Cards views.
3. Promote a generated artifact or review conversation-linked deletion choices.

## Notes

- Group and public generated artifact promotions can require approval before becoming searchable.
